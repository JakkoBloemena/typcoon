# ADR 003 — Budget ceiling confirmed at €50/month; domain renewal accepted as auto-renew

- **Date:** 2026-07-22
- **Decided by:** Shareholder (direct, /ceo channel conversation, 2026-07-22, during
  tick #1)
- **Authority citation:** Shareholder's own session — asked explicitly via the /ceo
  channel; answers: budget "Raise to €50/month"; domain "They will be automatically
  renewed. No need to worry about the pricing for it, it's cheap."

## Context

The charter carried a PROVISIONAL €25/month ceiling pending Shareholder confirmation
(charter.md, decisions/001-adoption.md). The spend ledger flagged the typcoon.com
renewal date and price as UNKNOWN — an untracked renewal.

## Decision

1. **Budget ceiling: €50/month**, replacing the provisional €25. Headroom is for a
   paid analytics tool or a free-tier upgrade without a fresh ask. The existing rules
   stand: any new recurring commitment is still recorded here and in metrics/spend.md
   at decision time; above the ceiling remains a Shareholder decision.
2. **Domain typcoon.com: auto-renews under the Shareholder's own registrar account;
   exact price immaterial by Shareholder statement.** The "to establish" obligation in
   metrics/spend.md is discharged — the monitor stops chasing it. Cancel-by condition
   unchanged (only on company death).

## Consequences

- charter.md budget section updated (no longer provisional).
- metrics/spend.md domain row updated; standing note removed.
- decisions/002 remains reserved for the payments ADR (assignment 002).
